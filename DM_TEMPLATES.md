# Instagram DM Outreach — Soft Reset

Manual DM scripts for the accounts in **`dm_worklist.csv`** (in-band creators who
have **no public email**, so the Brevo email pipeline can't reach them). DMing
these by hand is ToS-safe, converts well, and uses the ~97% of discovered
accounts the email channel throws away.

**Offer:** a free copy of *The 7-Day Child Screen Reset* ($7 PDF,
softresetbooks.com) → with the door open to an affiliate / paid collab.

---

## Sending rules (read once)

- **20–30 DMs/day max** from @softresetbooks. Cold DMs are the #1 cause of IG
  restrictions — slow and steady keeps the account safe. Never use a bot for this.
- **Personalize line 1 every time.** Use the `bio_hook` / `category` columns in
  the worklist. Generic = ignored. Personalized cold DMs get ~32% replies vs ~18%.
- **No link in the first DM.** Links in cold DMs trigger spam detection. Offer to
  send it *if they're interested* — drop the link only after they reply.
- **Work the list top-down by followers**, mark the `status` column
  (`sent` / `replied` / `passed`) so you don't double-DM.
- Keep it short. IG DMs are read on a phone in 3 seconds.

`[first name]` = from `full_name`. `[hook]` = something specific from `bio_hook`.

---

## Tier 1 — Nano (5k–20k followers): warm, gifting, tiny ask

> Hi [first name]! Your [hook — e.g. "toddler activity reels"] are so good — clearly
> made by someone who actually gets the screen-time struggle 🙏
>
> I wrote a little guide called *The 7-Day Child Screen Reset* and I'd love to gift
> you a free copy, no strings. Want me to send it over?

**Follow-up (if no reply after ~4 days):**

> No worries if it's not your thing! Offer still stands on the free copy if you'd
> ever like it 🌱

## Tier 2 — Micro (20k–100k): gift + soft affiliate

> Hey [first name]! Been following your [hook — e.g. "gentle parenting"] content and
> the way you talk about screen time really lines up with something I made.
>
> It's a calm, no-shame 7-day guide for helping kids reset their screen habits. I'd
> love to send you a free copy — and if it resonates, I run a simple affiliate
> setup so you can earn from any you share. Cool if I send the details?

**Follow-up:**

> Totally get it if you're swamped! Happy to just send the free copy either way —
> want me to drop it here?

## Tier 3 — Mid (100k–250k): concise, professional, paid collab

> Hi [first name] — really admire your work with [hook / audience]. I'm the founder
> of Soft Reset; we make *The 7-Day Child Screen Reset*, a parent guide that's been
> resonating with families navigating screen-time.

> I'd love to explore a paid collab or an affiliate partnership with you. Could I
> send over a free copy and a short partnership outline? Whatever's easiest for you.

**Follow-up:**

> Following up in case this got buried! Happy to send the guide + details whenever
> works — no pressure at all.
